How much does a website cost?
A basic business website starts from ₹12,000. A full web application with custom features ranges from ₹50,000 to ₹5,00,000+. Contact us for a free quote tailored to your specific needs and budget.
How long does development take?
A standard website takes 1–2 weeks. A custom web app takes 4–8 weeks. Mobile apps typically take 6–12 weeks. We always share a detailed timeline before starting so there are no surprises.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Yes! All projects come with 30 days of free support after launch. We also offer affordable monthly maintenance plans for ongoing updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization.
Can you work with my existing website?
Absolutely. We can redesign, enhance, or add new features to your existing website or application. We start with a thorough audit and then recommend the best approach forward.
What is your payment structure?
We work on a 50% advance + 50% on delivery model. For larger projects, milestone-based payments are available. All transactions are transparent with proper documentation and invoices.
Do you sign NDAs and provide code ownership?
Yes. We sign NDAs to protect your business idea. Upon project completion and full payment, you receive 100% ownership of all source code, designs, and assets — no strings attached.
