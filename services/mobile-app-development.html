Tech Comparison

Flutter vs React Native vs Native

We help you pick the right technology for your app — no bias, just the right fit.

Flutter — Our Primary Choice

Single codebase → Android + iOS. Near-native performance, beautiful custom UI, fastest development time. Best for 90% of projects.

React Native

JavaScript-based cross-platform. Great for teams already using React. Huge ecosystem, OTA updates possible, strong community.

Native Android / iOS

Maximum performance, full hardware access. Best for complex apps needing deep platform integration — Kotlin for Android, Swift for iOS.
